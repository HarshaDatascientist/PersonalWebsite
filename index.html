About Me

Dr. Harsha A. Bhute

Associate Professor

Department of Information Technology

Dedicated, enthusiastic individual with outstanding academic, communication, organizational, and people skills and a passion for teaching with 22+ years of experience. Completed Ph.D. (Computer Science and Engineering) specialization in mobility management in heterogeneous wireless networks. Experise in system programming, computer networks, wireless networking, data science, machine learning and computer vision. As per the growth of industry and teaching practices, upgraded myself by completing certification of AWS Cloud Foundation, Python Programming, Neo4J, SciLAB and Neural networks & Deep Learning. Committed to offer interested students my full attention and expertise to help further their academic and professional goals.
